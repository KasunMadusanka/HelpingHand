Some more detail about Helping Hand project and what is this site doing.

Our aspiration is to help the people who need financial support in different conditions such as health conditions like surgeries, education, constructions (roads, bridges, schools, etc). A person can donate not only money, but also can sacrifice their time for an event like repairing a school, temple etc.This system is acting as an inter meddler for introducing different donors to different paupers and different paupers to different donors.
